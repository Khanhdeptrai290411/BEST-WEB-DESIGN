Forest Fires

Forest fires have always been a worrisome issue, stemming from various causes. One of them is the lack of awareness among local residents about the importance of forest protection, leading them to burn forests for timber. Another cause is illegal logging by criminals. The consequences are devastating, resulting in record-breaking wildfires and significant damage to biodiversity.
